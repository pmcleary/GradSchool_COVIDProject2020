Attribute_Accuracy:
Attribute_Accuracy_Report:
Indiana Geological Survey metadata reports-

"All county names contained in the fields named "NAME_U" and "NAME_L" were checked against the USGS 7.5-minute quadrangles. The prefix code contained in the field named "NCAPC" was checked against the AccessIndiana Indiana Bureau of Motor Vehicles license plates and titles county prefixes listed on license plates. US Census Bureau makes no claim as to the accuracy of its SF1 table attributes. See Census Technical Documentation for more information."

Logical_Consistency_Report:
Indiana Geological Survey metadata reports -

"A check was made for dangling nodes and none were detected."

Completeness_Report:
Indiana Geological Survey metadata reports -

"A check was made to verify that the county boundaries are continuous and are identical to the county boundaries contained in IN_LANDSURVEY. Census SF1 table attributes were joined for all counties."
